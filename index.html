About Me

My name is Katie Ediger and I am a User Experience (UX) Librarian at the Illinois Institute of Technology in the Paul V. Galvin Library. I try to make the library's services and spaces more responsive to everyone's needs. As a librarian I spend a lot of my time helping people connect with and understand technology. At IIT, and previously at Northeastern Illinois University, I use Drupal to manage the library's web presence.
